About

Warhaniye offers a unique combination of bespoke luxury villas and the adrenaline rush of the fight for survival in a war-torn countryside.

  • A Villa is Born

    In a remote fishing village that is a mere 2100km from Baghdad, a British father of two foolheartedly enlists a local architect to design a luxury villa for his family. To this day the unknown British oligarch denies that building with such intimate proximity to Syria (1500km) and North Korea (8000km) was inviting inevitable chaos.
